DE@Paul Klee war Linkshänder. Er konnte aber beidhändig malen. Viele seiner Bauhaus-Studenten waren so tief beeindruckt von den künstlerischen Fähigkeiten des passionierten Malers, dass sie ihm eigene Werke widmeten. Oft als infantil verspottetet, trafen Klees reduzierte Gemälde den modernen Nerv der Zeit.
EN@Paul Klee was left-handed. But he could paint with both hands. Many of his Bauhaus students were so deeply impressed by the artistic skills of the impassioned painter that they dedicated their own works to him. Often derided as infantile, Klee’s reduced style of painting caught the modern spirit of the times.
